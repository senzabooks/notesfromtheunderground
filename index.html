“Issues with sequencing” is a small website that I built using only html and css. It exists a response to the Notes from the Underground assignment. It has three main parts that explore my associations to sequencing in various ways. The left section is focused on the relationship between typography in time based works. It includes parts of interview with Hollis Frampton, a structural film pioneer. The middle column is a selection of my references and associations with the topic of sequencing and connections between things. The right part is a visual exploration of encoding the same message with various symbols.
